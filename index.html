About Us

Authentic Mexican flavors in Dalton

Tacos Beto is your neighborhood spot for authentic Mexican tacos and comfort food in Dalton, Georgia. We serve house-made tortillas de maíz and harina, fresh cilantro and onion, and your choice of meat in every bite. Whether you're craving street-style tacos, a hearty burrito, or a cheesy quesadilla, we've got you covered. Come visit us and taste the difference — Bienvenidos!
